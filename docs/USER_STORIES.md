# SailBook — User Stories

## Admin (Andy)

### Dashboard
- AS-1: I log in and see a dashboard with upcoming courses, enrollment counts, and sessions this week.

### Course Catalog
- AS-2: I create and edit course types (ASA 101, Open Sailing, Dinghy Sailing) — the catalog of what we offer.
- AS-3: I deactivate course types we no longer offer without deleting historical data.

### Course Management
- AS-4: I create a course offering by selecting a course type, assigning an instructor, setting capacity and price, and adding one or more sessions with dates/times/locations.
- AS-5: I edit an existing course — change instructor, capacity, price, add/remove sessions.
- AS-6: I cancel an entire course and see which students are affected.

### Session Management
- AS-7: I cancel a specific session (weather day), enter a reason, and see affected students.
- AS-8: I create a makeup session attached to a course and assign students who missed a cancelled session.
- AS-9: I swap the instructor on a specific session without changing the course default.

### Enrollment & Attendance
- AS-10: I view enrollments per course — who's registered, their status, contact info.
- AS-11: I confirm or cancel a student's enrollment.
- AS-12: I take attendance for a session — mark each student as attended, missed, or excused.
- AS-13: I see which students have missed sessions and whether they've completed makeups.

### User Management
- AS-14: I add and deactivate instructors.
- AS-15: I view the student list with contact info and enrollment history.

## Student (Jane)

### Account
- ST-1: I create an account with my name, email, phone, and experience level.
- ST-2: I log in and land on my student dashboard.

### Browsing & Registration
- ST-3: I browse available courses — see course type, dates/times, instructor, price, and spots remaining.
- ST-4: I register for a course.
- ST-5: I cannot register for a course that is full.
- ST-6: I cannot register for the same course twice.

### My Courses
- ST-7: I see my enrolled courses with upcoming session dates/times.
- ST-8: I see my past attendance — which sessions I attended, missed, or was excused from.
- ST-9: I see available makeup sessions if I missed one.

### Future (V2)
- ST-10: I cancel my own enrollment. (Deferred — ships with payment processing. For V1, student contacts admin to cancel.)

## Instructor (Captain Dave)

### Dashboard
- IN-1: I log in and see my assigned courses and upcoming sessions.
- IN-2: I see my schedule — a list of upcoming sessions with dates, times, locations.

### Roster & Attendance
- IN-3: I view the roster for each of my sessions — who's expected, attendance status.
- IN-4: I see which students are makeups from other courses.

### Future (V2)
- IN-5: I add notes to a session after teaching it (for instructor continuity).
