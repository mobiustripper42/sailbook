Learn to sail on Lake Erie.

Cleveland's community sailing school. From your very first time on the water to ASA certification — we meet you where you are, and take it from there.

2026 season · May – September · Edgewater Marina, Cleveland
Why sail with us

No experience assumed. None required.

This school is about meeting people where they are in their sailing journey — not gatekeeping, not assuming. Whether you've never touched a tiller or you're chasing a bareboat cert, there's a boat for you.

Start anywhere

Never sailed? Open Sailing and Dinghy Basics get you on the water in an afternoon. No jargon, no pressure.

◎

Real certification

ASA 101 through 104, taught by USCG-licensed captains. Earn credentials recognized anywhere you charter.

⌖

Right downtown

We sail out of Edgewater Marina — ten minutes from downtown Cleveland, with the skyline off your stern.
